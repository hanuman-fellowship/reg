Rentals

A call comes for a proposed rental.

Fill out a Proposal

First choose Rentals from the top menu. Then choose the Proposals link. Then Add.
  • Contact Person
    Note the required fields. On leaving the last name field, Reg will fill in the rest from prior data, if it is available.
  • IF the Contract Signer is different from the contact person there is a link 'Contract Signer?' which will open up an additional area to enter the info. This also auto fills from prior data.
  • Max
  • Dates requested
    This can be as verbose and as vague as you wish.
  • Default check in is 4:00, default check out is 1:00 You CAN put hh:mm for minutes. Like 4:30. No need to enter am/pm because it is assumed that 8:00 is am and 7:00 is pm. This is okay, yes?
  • Program meeting date is important.
  • Deposit field is required.

The several multiline text fields have a fancy editor for Word-like formatting.

After entry you see the list of all proposals. They are listed in order of program meeting date. Only current or future ones are shown Can search for others by the group name. * is wildcard Can edit/update/delete them as usual.

The program meeting then happens and decisions are made to approve or deny the proposals.

Deny

If deny, Choose Edit, check deny and possibly put a reason in Miscellaneous Notes. Then click 'Transmit' to move contact person (and contract signer) to People for later reference.

Create a Rental

If approve - click 'Approve'. This brings up a Rental creation screen (in a new window) with these fields already filled in from the information in the proposal:
  • Check in, Check out
  • Tentative
  • Rental Name (the proposal group name)
  • Contact Person/Contract Signer come over
  • Max
  • Deposit
Note that the Rental screen has 4 tabs:
  1. Meals & Lodging
  2. Misc
  3. Finance
  4. Web
Fill in start, end date and hit Submit. A rental is thus created.

The Rental's Summary is automatically created. Note that Special Needs, Food Service, Leader Housing, Misc Notes, Provisos, etc are taken directly from the Proposal.

The Contact Person and Contract Signer in the proposal are automatically 'transmitted' to the People table with affiliation 'Proposal Submitter'.

After a Proposal is 'Approved' you can no longer modify it. You can go back and forth between the Proposal and the Rental with links (To Rental/To Proposal) on each screen. Similarily, after the contact person (and contract signer) have been 'Transmitted' you cannot modify them on the Proposal. Instead, you modify their info in the People screen. From the Proposal and the Rental you can get to the people screen in one click on a link.

Rentals can also be created directly without going through the proposal process. Choose 'Add' on the Rentals list screen. One difference is that you need to add the contact person separately in People and then retype their name after choosing 'Update' next to the Contact Person field in Rentals. Similarily for contract signer if not the contact person.

Several other things need to be done to make the rental complete:

  • Choose the appropriate housing cost. Note that it cannot be the default - the default is for Programs and Personal Retreats not Rentals. If the rental will be having lunches the housing cost should reflect this - the housing cost name must contain the word 'lunch'.
  • Assign meeting places and breakout places.
  • Choose which days have lunches. Note that if the rental begins after 1:00 they cannot have lunch on the first day.
  • Verify the GL Number which was automatically computed.
  • Enter any comment and web information, if provided. The web stuff will appear on the Events Calendar at www.mountmadonna.org. Some organizations won't want their information public but some will. From an MMC perspective I think it is nice to be able to show that there are many other things happening at the center.

MMC Does Registration and Parallel Programs

The item 'MMC Does Registration' is normally unchecked.

For special rentals (like AVI and OGS) MMC does the individual registration for them but it is still a Rental - from a financial and contractual perspective. In order to do the registrations and generate a web page on which people can register online you will need to create a parallel 'Program' with the same name and the same dates as the 'Rental'. You will not be able to assign any meeting spaces or breakout spaces to this program. This program will not appear on the calendar at all - not even in the 'No Where' place. The original rental will appear.

To help you to not forget to create this parallel program newReg will bring you to a Program creation dialog when you first check 'MMC Does Registration' on the Rental screen. The name and dates will be filled in from the rental. The rest of the program attributes (including the web page information) is up to you. Do not change the name or the dates! You can but you would only confuse yourself and others!

Once the parallel program is created you can easily go back and forth between the rental and its program with the 'To Program' and 'To Rental' links.

In the lists of all rentals and programs these special rentals and their parallel programs will be marked with a percent '%'

Contract

Once the rental is complete you can generate a contract by simply choosing 'Contract' and printing it. This is a legal document and hence needs to be examined and vetted carefully. Double check the housing costs. Seminar house use affects the contract. Lunches do, too. Try the various combinations. Read the meal times carefully.

Generating the contract will put the current date in the 'Contract Sent' field and change the status from 'Tentative' to 'Sent'. The contract should be sent to the contact person for signature and a request for a deposit check.

When the contract is received back the rental should be edited to put the date of contract reception in the Contract Received field. The deposit check should also be entered as a Payment (in the Finance tab). The rental status is now 'Received'.

Each status of a rental has its own color - which can be changed by the super_admin in Configuration/Strings. This color is prominently visible when viewing the rentals and also as a border color in the Calendar.

Arrangements

The MMC Rental Coordinator can now begin the conversation about the arrangements - all the things in the Summary. There is an ongoing conversation about how many people will be coming and what kind of housing they will want. This information is entered into the Meals/Lodging tab of the Rental.

Entire housing clusters can be booked (or cancelled) for a rental in one fell swoop.

When booking rooms individually you can select more than one at a time from the select list by holding down the control key while clicking the room names - and then pressing Submit.

On the Meals/Lodging tab to the right of the number of people there is a color:

Green
More rooms need to be picked to adequately cover the housing needs.
Red
Too many rooms have been picked. You can delete some by clicking on their name.
White
All is covered just right.

Post Rental

The Rental comes and all hopefully goes well. At this point the coordinator asks the contact person the question - 'What Happened?' This is when we find out that some people came late, some left early. This affects the 'Attendance' field for each housing type. It can get very complicated dealing with the shifting numbers of people in the rooms. There is a help link 'How?' near the Attendance field that explains a few things of importance.

If there were some people that did not attend the entire rental this fact will be reflected in the number at the bottom entitled 'Days of Non-Attendance'. This is like a 'check number' to help you verify that you entered the multiple 'people x days' terms correctly.

The coordinator can choose 'Invoice' to get a nicely laid out and accurate invoice to print for the contact person. Actually, it is as accurate as the input data.

A check is made out for the unpaid balance and it is entered as a Payment on the Rental. The status of the Rental changes to 'Done'.

If the payment was not made on the last day of the Rental the status becomes 'Due' - with its own unique color to make it stand out.
