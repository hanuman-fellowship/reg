PRESENTER BIOtop

KAMALESH GINGER HOOVEN, E-RYT500, Certified Ayurvedic Practitioner. You may know Kamalesh as lead coordinator and an instructor in Mount Madonna's Yoga Teacher Trainings. But before arriving at Mount Madonna in 2002, Kamalesh was living in Guatemala since 1986 where she was an entrepreneur whose goal was to create rightful employment under the yogic principles of aparigraha (non-greediness) and ahimsa non-harming).

In 2000 she opened the first local yoga studio in La Antigua Guatemala to share the practices and the theories behind them. She quickly realized she wanted more knowlege. That brought her to Mount Madonna Center in the spring of 2002 to take a 700 hour Yoga Teacher Training with author and scholar Georg Feuerstein. While she completed the 700 hour training with Feuerstein and studied with notables like Patricia Walden, John Friend, Judith Lasater, Janice Gates, and Roger Cole, Kamalesh felt she had found her true teacher and mentor in Baba Hari Dass.

Following Babaji's example, Kamalesh began to study Ayurveda to complement her understanding of yoga. She incorporates her love for anatomy and physiology as well as Ayurveda into all of her teaching.

Holy Week in La Antigua Guatemala has a special place in Kamalesh's heart. There is an open display of love and devotion unlike any other. She looks forward to sharing this amazing experience while integrating daily sadhana, asana and contemplation.
