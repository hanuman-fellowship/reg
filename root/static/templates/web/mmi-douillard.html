Dr. John Douillard

Healing The Cause of Anxiety and Depression with Ayurveda
Releasing the Power & Joy of Your Soul
August 28-30, 2009

In this stressed-out society, many of us suffer from anxiety and depression. Ayurveda teaches us to create ease and balance by honoring our natural cycles and disarming the fight-or-flight response. Along with mild herbs and treatments, learn lifestyle changes that allow us to float naturally downstream instead of going against the current. A recent preliminary study showed that a balanced lifestyle can significantly decrease anxiety, depression, cravings, fatigue, insomnia, and weight gain in just two weeks. Come get inspired to make small changes that will make a big difference so you can release the power and joy of your soul.

  • Learn about your Ayurvedic mind-body type and how to support your unique predispositions
  • Discover the best herbs, daily routines, exercise, yoga practices, and breathing techniques to resolve your difficulties.
  • Learn how to eat, what to eat, and when to eat according to your body type and find out why certain eating habits influence your moods.
  • Make your sensitivity the source of your power rather than a weakness.



Dr. John Douillard is an Ayurvedic and chiropractic physician who has taught Ayurvedic medicine, natural health, fitness, and nutrition internationally for over 22 years. He is also author of The 3-Season Diet; Body, Mind, and Sport; Perfect Health for Kids, The Encyclopedia of Ayurvedic Massage and three Ayurvedic DVD's recently released by Gaiam on Stress, Detox and Weight Loss. He was the former Director of Player Develoment for the New Jersey Nets in the NBA and co-directed an Ayurvedic Cancer Center with Deepak Chopra for 8 years. Dr. Douillard trained in India and received his PhD in Ayurvedic Medicine. He currently directs the LifeSpa - Ayurvedic Retreat Center in Boulder, CO, where he lives with his wife and six children. www.lifespa.com
