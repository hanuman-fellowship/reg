Personal Retreats

Explore the many hiking trails, attend daily yoga classes, soak in the hot tub surrounded by the beautiful madrone forrest and enjoy nourishing vegetarian meals. Retreat from your busy daily life and connect with your center again.

Individuals, groups, and families are welcome to sojourn with us before or after a group event, or at another time when some unique rest and re-creation are wanted. The Center provides a peaceful environment to refresh and recharge, as well as lodging, and vegetarian meals. You are welcome to participate in our ongoing classes, to swim, hike, hot tub, and meditate.

We ask all of our guests not to bring food or beverages, alcohol or other drugs, or pets. Incense, candles and other flammables are not allowed except for program related rituals, as the risk of fire to our wood-and-grassland is great. As a courtesy to other guests, who may have chemical sensitivities, please don't wear scented body products.

Prices are per person per day and include meals, lodging and facilities use. If another person is coming with you, please make sure you register them separately.

Housing is available in both rooms and in cabins.

Rooms are in hotel-style buildings (no phones or TVs in rooms, however). Some rooms include a private bathroom with shower. Others have access to nearby shared (but private while in use) bathrooms and showers.

Cabins are located in a forest grove, and include electricity and heat, but not indoor plumbing.  A separate shower house is nearby; it has private showers and dressing rooms, and flush toilets.

All beds are twin size.

Mount Madonna Center tents, available from May 1 through October 31, have access to showers and flush toilets or privies, and are set up with 2 foam mattresses, but no bedding.

Developed campsites, for your own tent, with good drainage and access to showers and flush toilets or privies, are available year round. Please be aware that we can receive significant amounts of rain from November through May.

Various spots are available for your van, camper or other vehicle. Some have access to electricity, but no other hookups. Showers and restrooms are available in campgrounds and Center buildings.

Balance is due on arrival (Visa, MasterCard, cash or check only).

For cancellations received at least two weeks before your personal retreat, half of the required deposit will be charged as a processing fee, and half will be credited to any other program within one year. There is a $20 fee for housing changes. No credit or refund is available if you give less than two weeks notice prior to the start date, if you do not show up, or if you leave an event early.
