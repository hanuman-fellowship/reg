Distributed Demographic Update

This document describes a mechanism within Reg that makes it very easy to keep everyone's address, email, phone numbers, etcetera up to date. This is done by requesting that people to do it themselves. This distributes the clerical burden of our staff and reduces the risk of typos and errors.
  1. The process is begun by Adding a Report (Listings > People > General Reports) with a format of type 'Email, Code' or 'Address, Code'. 'Email, Code' is used for making the requests by email. 'Address, Code' is used when sending requests by snail mail.

    The other attributes of the Report are up to you. They select a subset of all people by restricting the zip code, the affiliations, and/or the date of last contact. In both cases, people who are Inactive will not be included in the report's output.
