COVID-19 Vaccinations

Starting on September 1, 2021 everyone coming to MMC is required to have been fully vaccinated for COVID-19.

This requirement affects Programs and Rentals in different ways.

Rentals

Rental coordinators (the Contact Person and/or the Contract Signer) are tasked with verifying that the people they invite have been fully vaccinated. This involves a level of trust.

Programs

Programs that are ONLINE are, of course, exempt from the vaccination requirement because the attendees will not be at MMC.

Programs that will meet at MMC (including Personal Retreats and Special Guests) will require that the registrants prove that they have been fully vaccinated. This means that they will need to upload an image of their vaccination card.

The starting point is the 'COVID Vaccination Required' attribute on the Misc tab of Programs. Turning that on will trigger other things - like the little black 'v' next to the program title in the list of programs. The little green 'w' means that the program is 'web ready' and will be exported to mountmadonna.org.
