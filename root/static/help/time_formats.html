Entering Times

Times consist of hours and minutes and can be entered in these formats:
    032524 hour time = 3:25 am
    152524 hour time = 3:25 pm
    122524 hour time = 12:25 pm
    002524 hour time = 12:25 am
    33:00 defaults to p.m.
    3:253:25 defaults to p.m.
    3253:25 defaults to p.m.
    99:00 defaults to a.m.
    9:259:25 defaults to a.m.
    1010:00 defaults to a.m.
To summarize:
If you enter 4 digits it is 24 hour time. If you enter 1, 2, or 3 digits (or include a colon :) it is 12 hour time and the convention is that 8 is a.m. and 7 is p.m. This works because the early morning times and after dinner is a time when not much is scheduled to start. Exceptions to this rule can be dealt with by entering 4 digits.

You CAN also append a 'am' or 'pm' to assert the am/pm status.
