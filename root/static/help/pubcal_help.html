This is how to navigate the MMC calendar of future events:

To the right of each month name there are yearly jump images. Hovering over them you will see month names. When you click you will jump to that month. This can be easier than scrolling.

There are 'Details' checkboxes with which you can get more details of the events in that month.

Each meeting place has a unique color and is assigned a row position within the calendar.

There are Programs, Rentals, and Events. Programs have a registration count. Rentals have a maximum and a reserved count.

At the bottom of each month you will see numbers on a blue background. These are the number of Personal Retreatants at the center on that day.
