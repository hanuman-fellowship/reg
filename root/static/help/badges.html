Badges

Badges are used for a variety of purposes:

  • As a name tag.
  • To give important information like the emergency number and the gate code.
  • At the front of the meal line the badges are checked to verify someone has paid for meals.
  • To remind the person which room they're in.

You print the badges two-sided in color, cut them up with a paper cutter, and insert them into plastic sleeves with lanyards. After the badges are printed you can Close the window with the badges.

Badges can be printed from several places:

  • The Listings page - to print badges for PRs and Special Guests.
  • A Rental page
  • The AllRegs page for Programs
  • An individual Registration page
  • Custom badges: see Configuration > Badge

Since Personal Retreats (and Special Guests) programs have a variety of arrival dates you cannot print badges from the AllRegs page of these 'programs'.

Badges require that the program or rental have a gate code in the Summary. They also require that the Badge Title or Title (in the Web tab) - be no more than 30 characters in length. If any of these things are not present the generation of badges will be aborted.
