Helpful Hints for Filling in the Housing Grid

  1. The Name field should only contain names of the people staying in the room.
  2. The Email and Notes field should contain the email address of the person. If the person truly has no email address put the words 'no email' somewhere in the field. You can also use this field for whatever other purpose you wish.
  3. In a Name field of the Housing Assignment Grid if you put:
      Charles Jones & Mary Smith
    (names separated by an ampersand '&') this means that there are two people in the space (usually a tent) and that they both should be charged the normal cost.
  4. If you put:
      Mark Bronson & Aaron child
    or
      Greta Haroldson & Annie child & Bobby child
    (a name followed by the word 'child') then the children will be charged half of the normal cost. Children are defined to be age 12 or under.
  5. If the name is like this:
      James Carter (Jimmy)
    Then the badge will have just the nickname 'Jimmy'.
  6. If you put:
      Mark Gibson / Edward Unger
    (names separated by a slash '/') this means that Mark and Edward will both occupy the room sometime during the event but not at the same time.
  7. The above cases will be highlighted in red when the grid is displayed with costs.
  8. The names of houses with a fixed cost will be shown in green.
