In a name field of the Housing Assignment Grid if you put:
    Charles Jones & Mary Smith
(names separated by an ampersand '&') this means that there are two people in the space (usually a tent) and that they both should be charged the normal cost.

If you put:

    Mark Bronson & Aaron child
or
    Greta Haroldson & Annie child & Bobby child
(a name followed by the word 'child') then the children will be charged half of the normal cost. Children are defined to be age 12 or under.

The above cases will be highlighted in red when the grid is displayed with costs.

The names of houses with a fixed cost will be shown in green.
