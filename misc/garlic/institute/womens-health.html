Ayurveda for Women's Health

September 21, 2013
Instructor: Rucha Kelkar, BAMS, MPT

Spend one day learning healthful living through diet and lifestyle as well as preventative measures for women's health disorders. Includes discussion of diseases affecting women such as depression, osteoporosis, stress, premenstrual syndrome, menopause and fibroids. Learn the uses of Ayurvedic herbs as they pertain to women's health.
